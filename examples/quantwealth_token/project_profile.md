# Project Profile — QuantWealth Token (QWT)

## Executive Summary

QuantWealth Token (QWT) is a Stack Token powering the QuantWealth AI platform — an AI-powered trading insights platform for NSE stocks. QWT holders gain access to premium AI trading signals, reduced backtest fees, and governance voting rights. The token is formed on the HACD Stack Protocol, requiring 50 HAC per HACD lot as formation cost.

## Problem Statement

Retail traders lack access to institutional-grade AI analysis. Existing trading platforms are expensive, lack AI capabilities, or provide generic signals that don't account for individual risk profiles. QuantWealth AI addresses this by providing personalized AI-powered trading insights at an affordable cost.

## Solution

QuantWealth AI uses AI models to analyze NSE stock data, generate trading signals, and provide backtesting capabilities. QWT token holders unlock premium features including:
- Access to AI-generated trading signals with conviction levels
- 50% discount on backtest fees
- Governance voting on platform improvements
- Future staking rewards and cross-chain access

## Why HACD (PoW Fit)

QuantWealth Token uses HACD Stack Protocol because:
1. **PoW narrative alignment**: AI-powered trading (computational work) pairs naturally with PoW asset formation
2. **Formation cost credibility**: Participants must commit 50 HAC per HACD lot, signaling genuine commitment
3. **Supply discipline**: Fixed supply of 1M tokens tied to 100 HACD lots prevents inflation
4. **Launchpad visibility**: HACD Launchpad provides targeted exposure to PoW-focused community
