# Issuer FAQ — QuantWealth Token (QWT)

## General Questions

**What is QuantWealth Token (QWT)?**
QWT is a Stack Token formed on the HACD Stack Protocol. It powers the QuantWealth AI platform, which provides AI-powered trading insights for NSE stocks.

**What is the utility of QWT?**
QWT holders gain access to premium AI trading signals, 50% discount on backtest fees, and governance voting rights on platform improvements.

**Why use HACD Stack Protocol?**
HACD Stack Protocol adds credibility through PoW formation cost, aligns with the AI computational work narrative, provides supply discipline, and offers Launchpad visibility to the PoW community.

## Participation

**How do I participate in the Stack formation?**
1. Acquire HAC from supported exchanges (CoinEx, Vindax, Dex-trade)
2. Acquire HACD from hacash.org/get
3. Visit hacd.it/launchpad on July 1, 2026
4. Enter 1-100 HACD lots to form QWT
5. Receive 10,000 QWT per HACD lot formed

**What is the formation cost?**
The formation cost is 50 HAC per HACD. This is the on-chain cost to form the Stack and serves as a formation reference.

**What is the minimum participation?**
Minimum 1 HACD lot (10,000 QWT + 50 HAC formation cost).

**What is the maximum participation?**
No maximum — up to 100 HACD lots available.

## Stack Mechanics

**What happens when I remove my Stack?**
When you remove your Stack, the HACD is released back to you, and the QWT tokens are burned (permanently removed from supply).

**Is there a guaranteed price?**
No. The formation cost of 50 HAC per HACD is a formation reference or cost basis reference, not a guaranteed floor price. Market dynamics will determine the actual trading price.

**What is the removal effect?**
Burn Asset — QWT tokens are permanently removed from supply when the Stack is removed.
