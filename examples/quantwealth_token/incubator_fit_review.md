# Incubator Fit Review — QuantWealth Token (QWT)

## Verdict: Strong Fit

## Why it fits HACD

QuantWealth Token benefits significantly from HACD as a PoW-native asset container. The project is an AI-powered trading platform, which aligns well with the PoW narrative — computational work (AI analysis) paired with PoW asset formation creates a coherent story. The Stack formation adds credibility to the token by requiring real cost (HAC) to participate, which signals commitment from holders.

## What HACD adds

1. **Credibility through formation cost**: Participants must burn HAC to form QWT, demonstrating commitment beyond simple token purchase
2. **PoW narrative alignment**: AI-powered trading (computational work) pairs naturally with PoW asset formation
3. **Supply discipline**: Fixed supply tied to HACD lots prevents inflation and creates scarcity
4. **Launchpad visibility**: HACD Launchpad provides targeted exposure to PoW-focused community

## Main concerns

1. **Utility at launch vs roadmap**: Need to ensure promised utility (premium signals, backtest discounts) is actually available at launch, not just roadmap items
2. **Team credibility**: Team background and experience should be clearly documented
3. **Market competition**: AI trading signals is a crowded space — need clear differentiation
4. **Regulatory considerations**: AI trading advice may attract regulatory scrutiny in some jurisdictions

## Required issuer confirmations

1. Confirm that premium AI signals and backtest discounts will be available at launch (not roadmap)
2. Provide team background and prior experience in AI/trading
3. Clarify jurisdiction and any regulatory considerations
4. Confirm formation cost (50 HAC per HACD) is intentional and sustainable

## Recommended next step

Proceed with full document generation. Ensure all launch-day utility is clearly documented and achievable. Address team background in project profile. Include regulatory considerations in risk review.
