## About QuantWealth Token

QuantWealth Token (QWT) is a Stack Token formed on the HACD Stack Protocol. It powers the QuantWealth AI platform — an AI-powered trading insights platform for NSE stocks. QWT holders gain access to premium AI trading signals, reduced backtest fees, and governance voting rights.

## Tokenomics

- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000 QWT
- **Asset Type**: Fungible Token (FT)
- **Stack Cost**: 50 HAC per HACD
- **Formation**: 100 HACD lots × 10,000 QWT per lot
- **Removal Effect**: Burn Asset

## Utility

## How to Participate

1. **Acquire HAC**: Obtain HAC from supported exchanges (CoinEx, Vindax, Dex-trade)
2. **Acquire HACD**: Mine or purchase HACD from hacash.org/get
3. **Form Stack**: Visit hacd.it/launchpad on July 1, 2026
4. **Participate**: Enter 1-100 HACD lots to form QWT
5. **Receive QWT**: 10,000 QWT per HACD lot formed

## Formation Cost

The formation cost is **50 HAC per HACD**. This is the on-chain cost to form the Stack and serves as a formation reference. This is not a guaranteed floor price — market dynamics will determine the actual trading price.
